About Me

My name is Matthew Roberts and I am a Web and Graphics Designer from Antioch, California. I hold a BFA in Graphic Design with a specialty in Design for Print along with a good knowledge of UX, UI, and Web Design. I currently own a business in Antioch known as mmroberts which provides design services for Celebrate Antioch Foundation and The Print Club. I am always looking for a new challenge, so do not hesitate to call me or email me! My Web Design Projects are a work in progress, so please return to this page in the future as new projects are added and the content below is updated.
